About Us

For a long time now, we have always been fascinated with the grandeur and the beauty of big celebrations like weddings, baby showers and birthdays. Not only because of the percious time spent with family and friends creating memories; but also by the pure atmostphere that is created by the use of decor and lighting, making the occassion much more special and intimate.

Here at NaiBella Creations we love to create authentic customized pieces for any of your decorating needs. We pride ourselves in creating unique and elegant designs that help our customers stand out in the crowd. We like to create a point of difference and will work tirelessly to make sure you have the perfect decorations for your event.

We are a family owned small business and we aim to help other families create everlasting memories by creating the perfect atmostphere at their special occassion.
